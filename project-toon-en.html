Operating Insight
I analyzed each post’s response and fed it into the next piece.

After every upload I check views, saves and shares, then repeat an improvement cycle. I confirmed with data that informational formats drive saves, storytelling drives empathy and shares, and CTA design drives profile-visit conversion.

What I Learned
01
Hooks are structure, not instinct

Repeatedly testing the first-3-second line, cut length and CTA placement, I confirmed that a well-performing structure is reproducible.

02
Saves & shares are the real metric

Collaboration results proved that saves, shares and profile visits — not raw views — are the metrics that drive the next action.

03
Proven formats transfer

Applying a hook structure proven on my personal account directly to a brand collaboration led to 890K views on a single piece.
